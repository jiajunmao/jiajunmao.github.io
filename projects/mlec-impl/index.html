Introduction#

We are currently working on implementing Multi-Level Erasure Code(MLEC) in HDFS and ZFS. We are going to implement C/C, C/D, D/C, D/D and all the repair schemes mentioned in the MLEC paper.

We are aiming for release by summer/autumn 2024.
